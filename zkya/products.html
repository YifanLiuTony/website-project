Foam Concentrate Agents 4 products

The chemistry inside every one of our suppression devices — plus three general-purpose foam concentrates for non-battery use cases.

Featured · Lithium-specific

Lithium-Battery Composite Micro-Foam Concentrate

A multi-phase composite formulation built for lithium-ion cell fires. Combines high-porosity fireproof micro-components, organic phosphorus flame retardants, specialty surfactants, and a high-density, low-conductivity suppression medium via a proprietary inorganic-organic compounding process.

Attributes: non-toxic, no persistent organic pollutants, no bioaccumulation — engineered for closed-environment battery use
AFFF

Aqueous Film-Forming Foam (AFFF)

Environmentally oriented AFFF forms a water film at the fuel-surface / air interface. Very low surface tension lets the foam rapidly spread and seal the surface against oxygen, suppressing re-ignition. For crude oil, petroleum, diesel, jet fuel and similar water-insoluble flammable liquids.

Typical use: fuel depots, aviation fueling, marine
AFFF / AR

Alcohol-Resistant AFFF (AFFF/AR)

Proprietary formula for polar-solvent / water-miscible flammable liquids. Optimised foaming forms an insulating layer on the fuel surface; very low surface tension spreads the agent while its penetration depth keeps it in place rather than flowing off. Stable foam structure also supports excellent burn-back resistance.

Typical use: alcohols, ketones, polar-solvent storage
Class A

Class-A Foam Concentrate

An environmentally oriented Class-A concentrate for ordinary combustible fires. Low surface tension and good penetration let the foam wet fuel quickly and soak into wood, textiles and deep-seated fires. Stable foam structure provides sustained burn-back resistance.

Typical use: warehouse, structural, Class-A suppression systems

High-Pressure Water Mist Systems 4 products

High-pressure water-mist (HPWM) hardware for facilities where water-based suppression is preferred. Fine mist rapidly absorbs heat, displaces oxygen, and scrubs combustion products — with minimal water volume and collateral damage.

HPWM · Pump unit

High-Pressure Water-Mist Pump Unit

Nine-plunger high-pressure pump supply, comprising plunger pump, jockey pump, motor, control cabinet, safety/pressure-sustain valve, check valve, piping, and regulated water tank. Available in domestic and imported series. Compact, uniform flow, multi-path safety relief protection.

HPWM · Zone valve

Zone Control Valve Box

Houses motor ball valve, manual ball valve, filter, pressure switch, pressure gauge, enclosure and fittings. Receives the signal from the fire-alarm controller, opens the motor valve, releases mist into the protected zone, and feeds back status via pressure switch. Auto / manual / mechanical emergency control modes.

HPWM · Open nozzle

High-Pressure Water-Mist Open Nozzle

Austenitic-stainless nozzle comprising shell and multiple centrifugal spray tips on a conical face. The swirl element inside creates rotating liquid that exits as a hollow-cone mist film — velocity differential with air breaks it into fine droplets. Wide atomisation angle, high momentum, good dispersion.

HPWM · Closed nozzle

High-Pressure Water-Mist Closed Nozzle

Closed/sealed variant of the centrifugal-mist nozzle — fires when its thermal element opens. Used in pre-action or wet-pipe HPWM systems where automatic localised release is required.
